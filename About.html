About NSUT-IIF

NSUT Incubation and Innovation Foundation (NSUT- IIF), founded in March 2016 as a section 8 company under "The Companies Act, 2013", is the incubator of Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT), Delhi. NSUT-IIF has its registered office on the NSUT campus located at Azad Hind Fauz Marg, Sector 3, Dwarka, Delhi, and it incubates startups of NSUT students, alumni, faculty, any other incubatee under the guidance of Government of NCT of Delhi at NSUT for two years with seed money/funding from NSUT-IIF.

Our aim is to create leading entrepreneurs and successful ventures by fostering innovative minds, young entrepreneurs, students, alumni and other members of the NSUT community and providing them with required training. mentorship. technical assistance, legal guidance, financial grants, working space, sharing knowledge and community networks.
